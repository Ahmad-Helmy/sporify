Manage your personal details

To change your email address, gender, country, or date of birth:

  1. Log in to your account page.
  2. Click EDIT PROFILE.
  3. Enter your new details and click SAVE.

Didn't work?

If you're unable to change your personal details, it's likely you signed up to Mayestro with Facebook. This means your Mayestro account registered the personal details associated with your Facebook account, and it's not possible to change those with Mayestro.

To change your personal details, you need to switch to a new account. See 'I want to use Mayestro without Facebook' for more information.
